Postank ("we", "us") is designed around a simple principle: your photos are processed on your own device, not on our servers. This page explains exactly what that means in practice, including the one feature that's an exception, the third-party services involved, and how advertising on this site works.

What we don't collect

We do not operate a server that receives your photos. Cropping, brightness/contrast adjustment, background removal, and the text/gradient card, product post, and collage features all run entirely in your browser using your device's own processing power. We never see, store, or have access to the photos you work with in Postank.

What's saved locally on your device

Your text entries and style choices (headlines, gradient picks, fonts, layout choices, and which tab you last used) are saved in your browser's local storage so you can pick up where you left off. This data stays on your device — it is never transmitted to us or anyone else. It is not saved for uploaded photos themselves, since photos are too large for this kind of storage. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's site data for this page.

The one feature that sends data off your device

The optional "Suggest captions" feature sends the photo you're working on to Google's Gemini API (via a small server we operate solely to keep our API key private) so it can suggest caption text. This is the only feature in Postank where a photo leaves your device, and it only happens when you explicitly tap that button. Google's own terms govern how that data is handled on their end; see Google's Gemini API terms for details.

Third-party services we rely on

Even though we don't run our own data-collecting server, your browser does make requests to a few third parties to load fonts and the on-device background-removal model:

  • Google Fonts — loads typefaces used across the site.
  • Google's MediaPipe model hosting / jsDelivr CDN — delivers the on-device background-removal model the first time you use that feature.

These providers may log standard request data (like your IP address) as part of normal web infrastructure, under their own privacy policies. We don't control or receive that data.

Advertising and cookies

This section applies once this site carries ads (e.g. via Google AdSense). If you're reading this before ads are live, treat it as forward-looking.

This site may show ads served by Google and its advertising partners. Google uses cookies — including the DoubleClick DART cookie — to serve ads based on your visits to this and other sites, in order to show ads that are more relevant to you. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings, or opt out of some third-party vendors' use of cookies via aboutads.info. You can read more about how Google uses data in the context of ads at policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

Children's privacy

Postank is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes — for example, when we add ads or a new feature that touches data differently — we'll update this page and the date above.
