About Postank

Postank is a free tool for getting photos ready to post — cropping and enhancing them for whichever platform you're using, removing backgrounds, building gradient text cards, and putting together product posts and collages.

The thing that makes it different from most tools like it: everything runs directly in your browser. There's no server processing your photos, no upload wait, and no account required. Your photo never leaves your device for any feature except the optional AI caption suggestions, which only run if you explicitly ask for them.

What you can do with it

  • Crop and resize photos to the exact dimensions each platform expects
  • Auto-enhance brightness and contrast based on the photo's own histogram
  • Remove or replace a photo's background
  • Build gradient text cards, with or without a photo behind them
  • Create product posts with price stickers and badges for small businesses
  • Combine several photos into a collage

Why it's free

Most of what Postank does — cropping, filters, background removal — runs as code in your browser rather than on a server, so there's no per-photo cost to cover. That's what makes it possible to offer for free.
